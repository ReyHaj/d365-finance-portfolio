** Core Concept (Simple Explanation)
A Trial Balance is a summary of all General Ledger accounts showing:

    Opening balance
    Debit movements
    Credit movements
    Closing balance
    If:
    Total Debit = Total Credit
    then the ledger is balanced and mathematically correct.

Phase 7 confirms that all operational transactions posted earlier resulted in a balanced ledger.
** Scope of Phase 7
Phase 7 focuses exclusively on General Ledger validation, including:

    Main account balances
    Debit and credit totals
    Cross-module posting effects (AP, AR, Inventory, Bank)
    Posting layer accuracy
