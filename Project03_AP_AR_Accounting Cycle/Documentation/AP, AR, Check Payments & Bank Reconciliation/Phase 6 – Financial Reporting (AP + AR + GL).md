*** Purpose of Phase 6 (WHY)

    Up to this point in the project, we executed multiple operational transactions, including:
    Vendor invoices and payments (AP)
    Customer invoices and settlements (AR)
    Bank postings
    Inventory movements
    However, executing transactions alone is not enough in a real finance environment.

The key business question is:

    Are all operational transactions correctly reflected in financial reports and the General Ledger?
    Phase 6 exists to answer this question.

Main objectives of Phase 6:

    Validate financial data accuracy after AP and AR cycles
    Ensure consistency between subledgers (AP / AR) and General Ledger (GL)
    Prepare reliable financial data for:

Trial Balance (Phase 7)
Inventory Value Reconciliation (Phase 8)
Cash Flow Analysis (Phase 9)

*** Phase 6 acts as the bridge between operations and financial reporting.
